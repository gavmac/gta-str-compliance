# Product Overview

## GTA Compliance Digest

A freemium SaaS platform that helps landlords and property managers stay compliant with municipal regulations across Greater Toronto Area cities.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Free Tier**: Citywide compliance digest emails when new regulations are published
- **Paid Tier**: Property-specific monitoring with deadlines, compliance scoring, and personalized reminders

### Target Market
- Landlords and property managers in GTA municipalities (Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham)
- Short-term rental (STR) and long-term rental (LTR) property owners
- Property management companies handling multiple properties

### Business Model
- Freemium with email-based engagement
- Goal: 100+ free subscribers and 5-10 paid subscribers within 90 days
- Revenue from paid subscriptions with property management features

### Key Features
- Multi-city compliance tracking
- Automated deadline calculations
- Compliance scoring system (0-100 points)
- Document management with expiration tracking
- Email notifications and personalized digests
- Administrative content management system

### Success Metrics
- User acquisition and conversion rates
- Email engagement and deliverability
- Property compliance scores and deadline adherence
- Subscription retention and churn rates
